# Bio
He is a research scientist at Meta. His current focus is to develop efficient and reliable systems for multimodal models and agents. In the past, I built scalable continuous learning systems and efficient edge-ai applications, such as video analytics and on-device talking-face synthesis. 

Previously, he was a postdoc at NYU systems group working with [Prof. Jinyang Li](https://www.news.cs.nyu.edu/~jinyang/). He obtained a computer science Ph.D. at New York University where he worked with [Prof. Sam Bowman](https://cims.nyu.edu/~sbowman/). Prior to joining NYU, he earned his M.Tech from Purdue University and B.Tech from Sichuan University. He collaborates closely with [Prof. Yuanchao Shu](https://yshu.org) from Zhejiang University, [Dr. Shiqi Jiang](https://chrisplus.me/) from Microsoft Research, and [Prof. Huanle Zhang](https://www.data-system.tech/) from Shandong University. He is the co-founder of [MengJiTech (孟及科技)](https://www.mengji.tech/index/) which is a startup focusing on AIGC.
